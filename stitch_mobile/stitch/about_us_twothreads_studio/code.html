OUR MISSION

We believe in the beauty of the handmade, and the peace found in the process.

At TwoThreads Studio, we source the finest natural materials and partner with master artisans to create embroidery kits that act as a bridge between traditional craft and modern mindfulness.

The Craftsmanship Process

How a TwoThreads kit comes to life.

STEP 01

Sourcing

We start with the fabric. Our unbleached linen is woven in a zero-waste facility in Belgium, ensuring perfect tension and a natural, earthy texture.

STEP 02

Design

Every pattern is hand-drawn by our global network of textile artists. We digitize these drawings carefully to retain the organic feel of the original sketch.

STEP 03

Curation

Threads are color-matched by eye, not by computer. We pack every box by hand in our studio, wrapping components in recycled paper.
