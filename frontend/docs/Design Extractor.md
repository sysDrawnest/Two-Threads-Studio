# Prompt — Extract Embroidery Artwork from Reference Image

## Objective

Extract **only the embroidered couple artwork** from the provided reference image while preserving its exact composition, proportions, pose, floral arrangement, and handcrafted embroidery aesthetic. Remove every surrounding object including the embroidery hoop, gift box, wrapping paper, shadows, wood surface, tissue paper, ribbons, lavender, background, and photographic elements, leaving only the embroidery design itself.

The final output should resemble a premium embroidery pattern, vector-ready artwork, or luxury design asset suitable for printing, embroidery digitization, branding, or product customization.

The artwork must remain faithful to the reference and **must not reinterpret, redesign, or invent new elements**.

---

## Design Extraction Requirements

Preserve exactly:

- Couple's pose and body proportions
- Wedding attire details
- Hair styling
- Facial orientation
- Hand placement
- Floral embroidery arrangement
- Botanical wreath composition
- Stitch flow
- Thread direction
- Layered embroidery texture
- Organic handmade imperfections
- Overall visual balance

Do **not** stylize, modernize, simplify, or alter the artwork.

---

## Cleanup Requirements

Completely remove:

- Embroidery hoop
- Linen background
- Kraft paper
- Gift box
- Tissue paper
- Twine
- Lavender
- Table
- Shadows
- Reflections
- Lighting gradients
- Background textures
- Camera perspective distortion
- Depth of field blur

The final artwork should appear perfectly flat and front-facing.

---

## Artwork Style

Maintain a handcrafted embroidery aesthetic while presenting the design as a clean, production-ready graphic.

The embroidery should feature:

- Crisp thread definition
- Clearly visible stitch directions
- Satin stitch texture
- Long-and-short stitch transitions
- Fine outline stitching
- Layered thread realism
- Natural cotton thread appearance
- Soft fabric realism
- Handmade imperfections
- Premium artisan craftsmanship

The result should feel suitable for:

- Premium embroidery patterns
- Wedding keepsake artwork
- Luxury embroidery kit packaging
- SVG tracing
- Vector recreation
- Laser engraving reference
- High-end print production

> **"This is an extraction task, not a generation task. Preserve the artwork exactly as it appears in the reference. Do not creatively modify or regenerate the design."**

That significantly increases the chances of getting an almost identical extracted design rather than a new interpretation.
