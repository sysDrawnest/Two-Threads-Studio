# TwoThreads Studio — Signature Embroidery Transformation Prompt

## Objective

Transform the uploaded customer photograph into a premium handcrafted embroidery artwork suitable for creating a luxury custom embroidery keepsake. Preserve the identity, facial features, pose, clothing silhouette, hairstyle, and emotional expression while translating the entire image into authentic thread embroidery. The result should resemble the work of a master embroidery artist, not an AI illustration or digital painting.

The final artwork must be production-ready for embroidery reference, premium custom gifts, wedding portraits, anniversary keepsakes, memorial portraits, family portraits, and home décor.

---

# Prompt

**Transform the uploaded customer photograph into a luxury handcrafted embroidery portrait while preserving the exact identity, facial structure, body proportions, pose, hairstyle, clothing silhouette, and emotional expression of every person in the image. Do not alter the composition or redesign the subjects. Recreate the photograph entirely using realistic embroidery techniques on premium natural ivory linen fabric. Every visible element should appear stitched using layered cotton embroidery thread with authentic satin stitches, long-and-short stitches, split stitches, back stitches, seed stitches, and delicate botanical detailing where appropriate. Maintain subtle handcrafted imperfections, realistic thread direction, layered stitch density, soft thread shadows, and tactile textile depth. The fabric should remain clean, flat, and wrinkle-free with no embroidery hoop visible. Place the finished embroidery artwork on a plain neutral ivory background with generous negative space surrounding the design. The overall aesthetic should feel timeless, elegant, artisan-made, emotionally meaningful, museum-quality, and suitable for a premium luxury keepsake by TwoThreads Studio. Ultra-photorealistic embroidery texture, visible thread fibers, premium craftsmanship, refined composition, studio presentation, 8K resolution.**

---

# Style Direction

The embroidery should feature:

- Premium cotton embroidery thread
- Natural ivory linen fabric
- Soft neutral color palette
- Realistic layered stitching
- Fine facial thread detailing
- Accurate clothing folds recreated with stitches
- Natural skin tones translated into thread
- Elegant thread shading
- Handmade artisan imperfections
- Museum-quality craftsmanship

---

# Background

- Plain ivory linen
- Flat studio background
- No embroidery hoop
- No table
- No props
- No wrapping
- No decorations
- No flowers (unless requested)
- No frame
- No shadows
- No distractions

---

# Camera / Presentation

- Straight-on orthographic view
- Flat-lay presentation
- Entire artwork visible
- Perfectly centered
- Plenty of whitespace
- Studio-quality lighting
- Crisp embroidery details

## One suggestion for your business

Instead of using the same embroidery style for every order, create a **"TwoThreads Signature Style."** Every customer photo is transformed into the same recognizable embroidery aesthetic—consistent thread style, stitch density, botanical accents (optional), neutral linen background, and refined color grading. Over time, customers will recognize a TwoThreads piece instantly, just as people recognize the visual style of luxury brands. That consistency becomes part of your brand identity.
