## MASTER AI PROMPT 01 ⭐

# Universal Product Hero (Clean Background) : IMAGE 1

Create an authentic luxury commercial product photograph featuring **a single handcrafted product** presented as the absolute hero against a clean, seamless solid background. The image should be visually indistinguishable from a professionally commissioned premium e-commerce and editorial product campaign, with absolutely no AI-generated appearance.

The product occupies approximately **75–85% of the frame**, centered with perfect visual balance and generous breathing room. Every material should display exceptional tactile realism, authentic surface texture, subtle natural imperfections, and true-to-life craftsmanship. Preserve fine details including stitching, woven fibers, thread texture, edges, seams, folds, structural form, handcrafted irregularities, and material depth.

The background is a seamless matte surface in a soft neutral tone such as warm off-white, light stone, muted cream, or gentle linen beige. It remains completely distraction-free with no gradients, props, furniture, decorative objects, architectural elements, or environmental storytelling. Only a subtle natural contact shadow grounds the product, giving it believable weight and physical presence.

Illuminate the product using soft diffused studio daylight created by a large overhead softbox and gentle side fill, producing clean highlights, realistic shadow transitions, accurate color reproduction, and exceptional material definition without harsh reflections. Lighting should reveal every handcrafted detail while maintaining a calm, premium appearance.

Photograph using a professional full-frame or medium-format digital camera with a **90mm macro or product lens at f/8**, maintaining edge-to-edge sharpness, accurate perspective, minimal optical distortion, realistic lens rendering, authentic color science, and high dynamic range. The final image should communicate premium craftsmanship, timeless elegance, elevated material realism, collector-quality presentation, and refined commercial product photography suitable for luxury websites, catalogs, packaging, and advertising.

**No text, logos, branding, labels, watermarks, graphic overlays, or unnecessary decorative elements.**

# MASTER AI PROMPT 02 ⭐

# Universal Macro Material & Craftsmanship Close-up : IMAGE 2

Create an authentic ultra-close commercial macro photograph focusing exclusively on the craftsmanship, material quality, and tactile surface of **a handcrafted product**. The image should feel like it was captured for a premium luxury product campaign, revealing the beauty of handmade construction through genuine photographic realism.

Frame an extreme close-up showing only the product's most important handcrafted details. Emphasize visible stitching, woven fibers, embroidery, thread texture, fabric weave, leather grain (if applicable), wood grain, ceramic texture, knitted structure, crochet work, macramé knots, surface transitions, seams, folds, edge finishing, and handcrafted imperfections that communicate genuine artisan quality. Every fiber should appear tangible enough to touch.

Maintain an extremely shallow yet physically accurate depth of field so the primary area of craftsmanship remains critically sharp while surrounding details transition naturally into a soft optical blur. The focus should immediately draw attention to the finest handcrafted details without appearing digitally sharpened.

The background should remain clean, seamless, and softly blurred in warm neutral tones, free from distractions, allowing complete visual attention to remain on the material itself.

Illuminate the product using soft directional daylight or diffused studio lighting that gently grazes across the surface, revealing dimensional texture, realistic thread depth, natural fabric behavior, authentic material aging, and subtle highlights without glare or artificial contrast.

Capture using a professional full-frame or medium-format camera with a **100mm macro lens at f/4**, preserving true optical rendering, realistic lens compression, exceptional micro-contrast, natural color reproduction, elevated material realism, and authentic photographic optics. The final image should communicate premium craftsmanship, tactile luxury, timeless artisan quality, and collector-level attention to detail while remaining completely photorealistic.

**No text, logos, branding, watermarks, human subjects, decorative props, or distracting background elements.**

---

## NEGATIVE PROMPT

AI-generated appearance, CGI, illustration, cartoon, plastic texture, synthetic fibers, unrealistic thread, blurry stitches, machine-made appearance, oversharpening, HDR glow, harsh reflections, noisy image, low resolution, compression artifacts, distorted fabric weave, unrealistic folds, duplicate stitches, fingerprints, dust, logos, branding, readable text, watermark, decorative props, background clutter, excessive color saturation, unrealistic lighting.
